There are a lot of types of pasta in Italy

Cannelloni

Cannelloni is a large tube-shaped pasta.

Conchiglie

These shell-like portions of pasta are available in a few different sizes.

(This type of pasta also works well in soup.)

Farfalle

These pasta portions bear a strong resemblance to small bow ties. Farfalle

Lasagne

Large, wide, flat sheets of pasta with ridged edges, lasagna is most famously used in the dish that bears the same name!
